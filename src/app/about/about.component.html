Kevin King

Founder

Based in Asheville, NC, breweries are a thing. Through many years of attending music venues, streaming music from home, the car, or subway, and an embarrassing amount of breweries visited, it occurred to me that I would love to hear what a brewery sounds like at any given time. An extension of the physical taproom, if you will.

Enter Tap'd. The ability to listen live to my favorite breweries around the country as well as to discover new breweries, beers and hopefully make new friends.
